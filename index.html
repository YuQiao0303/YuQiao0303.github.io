Biography

I'm currently a first-year CS graduate student at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, under the guidance of Xianzhi Li. Prior to this, I completed my B.Eng. (Honours) degree in Artificial Intelligence at Shandong University in 2023, during which I worked closely with Mengbai Xiao. I am deeply interested in the intersection of 3D Computer Vision, Graphics, Robotics, and 3D Deep Learning. More specifically, my areas of focus include:
  • 3D Scene Generation and Understanding
  • Embodied AI
  • 3D Shape Generation, Editing, and Reconstruction
  • Human-Scene Interaction
  • Human Motion Synthesis
My aspiration is to use the power of Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and Deep Learning techniques to construct a vivid and immersive science-fiction world that captures the imagination.
